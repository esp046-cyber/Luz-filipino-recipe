# Lutong Bahay - Authentic Filipino Recipes

A comprehensive web application featuring 200 traditional Filipino recipes with beautiful design, search functionality, and detailed cooking instructions.

## Features

- **200 Authentic Recipes**: Traditional Filipino dishes from all regions
- **Smart Search**: Search through recipe names, ingredients, and descriptions
- **Category Filtering**: Filter by Main Dish, Appetizer, Dessert, Beverage, or Snack
- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Detailed Instructions**: Complete ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and nutritional info
- **Cultural Design**: Warm Filipino-inspired color palette and typography
- **Interactive UI**: Smooth animations, modal popups, and intuitive navigation

## Recipe Categories

### Main Dishes (Most Popular)
- Adobo - The national dish of the Philippines
- Sinigang - Sour soup with tamarind
- Kare-Kare - Rich oxtail stew in peanut sauce
- Pancit Canton - Stir-fried wheat noodles
- Bicol Express - Spicy pork in coconut milk

### Appetizers
- Lumpia Shanghai - Crispy spring rolls
- Empanada - Meat-filled turnovers
- Isaw - Grilled chicken intestines
- Chicharon - Crispy pork belly

### Desserts
- Leche Flan - Silky custard with caramel
- Halo-Halo - Refreshing shaved ice dessert
- Bibingka - Traditional rice cake
- Cassava Cake - Moist cake with cassava

### Beverages
- Buko Juice - Fresh coconut water
- Calamansi Juice - Sour citrus drink
- Mais Con Yelo - Sweet corn with shaved ice
- Sago't Gulaman - Clear gelatin in syrup

### Snacks
- Taho - Silken tofu with syrup and tapioca
- Balut - Fertilized duck egg delicacy
- Turon - Caramelized banana spring rolls
- Polvoron - Powdered milk sweets

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Browse recipes by scrolling through the grid
3. Use the search bar to find specific dishes
4. Click category filters to view specific types of recipes
5. Click any recipe card to view detailed instructions
6. Press Escape or click outside the modal to close recipe details
